At Happy Tails Loving Care, I pride myself in caring for your pets just as much as you do. I offer flexible and personalized pet care, so you can have peace of mind and your pet can feel safe and happy while you're gone! From dog walks to overnight visits, I offer a variety of care options to ensure keeping your pets’ normal routine. I will provide a customized quote based on your specific care needs prior to providing services. Prices are subject to change.

½ of payment is due prior to start of services and serves as a non-refundable deposit. The remaining balance is due on the final day of services.

Payment Options: Cash, Zelle, PayPal, Venmo, and CashApp


Meet & Greet

This is the initial meeting where everyone gets acquainted, and we discuss your needs and my services. Here, I will meet and get to know your pet, learn your pet’s routine and see where items are located. I will also answer any questions you may have. At the meet, I will require 1 set of house keys and a deposit. The meet and greet usually takes about 30 minutes, depending on how many pets you have.
