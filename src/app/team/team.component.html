About Me

Nicole Welna

My name is Nicole Welna and I am the owner and primary care-taker at Happy Tails Loving Care. I live with my dog, Cassie, my two rats, Willow and Hazel, and my fish, Swim Shady. I've loved animals as long as I can remember, and nothing gives me more satisfaction than taking care of animals. I love all critters: big and small, all shapes and sizes. I grew up with dogs, cats, rabbits, fish and rats over the years. I care for all animals.

I’ve worked with animals in volunteer and paid positions and have had pets my entire life. I worked at a doggy day care over the years where I handled groups of 20+ dogs as well as handled fearful dogs on a one-on-one basis. I also volunteered with cats and dogs for over 10 years.

With Happy Tails, I get to wake up every day and do something I am extremely passionate about: taking care of animals while giving you peace of mind. I am insured and bonded through Pet Sitter Associates and Pet CPR and First Aid certified. In addition to this, I’ve taken animal science and behavior classes at Rutgers University while pursuing my bachelor’s degree in political science.

I’m an active walker and sitter. I want to become a part of your family and be involved. I want to be there for you and your pets. I want you to feel like you have someone to depend on, and that you're not alone in giving your babies the amazing lives that they deserve. If given the opportunity, I will do my absolute best to be the best sitter you and your pets have ever had.
